## Project Overview  
This project analyzes the performance of Blinkit retail outlets using Power BI. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales trends, outlet efficiency, customer preferences, and product performance.

The goal is to support data-driven decision-making by identifying high-performing outlets, understanding customer buying patterns, and highlighting areas for operational improvement.

## Business Problem  
Blinkit aims to optimize retail performance across multiple outlet tiers and formats while ensuring efficient inventory management and improved customer satisfaction.

Key challenges include:
- Over-reliance on specific outlet tiers for revenue generation  
- Inefficient inventory allocation across product categories  
- Underperformance of large outlet formats  
- Maintaining consistent sales during market disruptions  


## Tools  
- Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard development  
- Excel – Data cleaning and preprocessing  

## Dashboard Structure  

### 1. Executive Overview (Top-Level KPIs)  
- KPI Cards: Total Sales, Average Sales per Outlet, Number of Items, Customer Rating  
- Line Chart: Sales trend over time  

### 2. Outlet Performance Analysis  
- Funnel Chart: Sales distribution by outlet tier  
- Donut Chart: Sales contribution by outlet size  
- Bar Chart: Sales by outlet type  

### 3. Product & Category Insights  
- Bar Chart: Top-performing product categories  
- Matrix Table: Item performance and distribution  

### 4. Customer & Product Preferences  
- Donut/Bar Chart: Fat content preference (Regular vs Low-fat)  

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
Initial data exploration focused on:
- Understanding sales distribution across outlet tiers and sizes  
- Identifying trends and anomalies in yearly sales performance  
- Analyzing product category contributions to total revenue  
- Evaluating customer preference patterns  


## Data Analysis  
The analysis examined relationships between outlet characteristics, product categories, and overall sales performance.

Focus areas included:
- Sales trend analysis over time  
- Outlet tier and size efficiency comparison  
- Product category performance ranking  
- Customer preference impact on sales  


## Key Performance Indicators  

- **Total Sales:** $1.20M  
- **Average Sales per Outlet:** 141  
- **Number of Items:** 9K  
- **Average Customer Rating:** 4.0  


## Key Insights  

- Sales peaked around 2019, followed by a decline in 2020, indicating vulnerability to external disruptions.  

- Tier 3 outlets generated the highest sales (~$472K), outperforming Tier 1 and Tier 2 outlets.  

- Medium and small outlet sizes outperformed large outlets, showing better efficiency and scalability.  

- Fruits, snacks, household, dairy, and frozen items emerged as top-performing categories.  

- Regular fat products significantly outperformed low-fat options, reflecting stronger customer preference.  

- Supermarket Type 1 recorded the highest total sales ($787.55K), with strong consistency and item count.  
  Grocery stores, while generating lower total sales, showed higher item visibility, suggesting better product discovery.  


## Business Value  

This dashboard enables stakeholders to:
- Identify high-performing outlets and replicate success strategies  
- Optimize inventory allocation based on product demand  
- Improve operational efficiency across outlet formats  
- Monitor sales trends and prepare for market fluctuations  
- Enhance customer satisfaction through data-driven improvements  


## Recommendations  

- **Scale Efficient Outlet Models**  
  Expand medium and small outlet formats and replicate successful Tier 3 strategies across Tier 1 and Tier 2 outlets.  

- **Optimize Inventory Management**  
  Reduce underperforming product categories and prioritize fast-moving, high-demand items.  

- **Improve Operational Resilience**  
  Develop contingency strategies to manage demand shocks and external disruptions.  

- **Enhance Customer Experience**  
  Implement improvements to increase customer satisfaction and raise ratings beyond the current 4.0 average.  

- **Reassess Large Outlet Strategy**  
  Investigate inefficiencies in large outlets and redesign operational approaches to improve performance.
